# XAUUSD Walk-Forward Research — 2026-07-11

This report records the first cost-aware research run after live/backtest logic
was unified. It is an engineering validation artifact, not a recommendation to
trade or a promise of future performance.

## Dataset and assumptions

- Source: `data/xauusd_5m_merged.csv`
- Period: 2026-05-15 23:15 UTC through 2026-07-07 19:05 UTC
- Bars: 10,123; median interval: 5 minutes
- Data quality: 0 duplicate timestamps, 0 out-of-order timestamps, 34 gaps;
  largest gap 3,180 minutes
- Naive CSV timestamps are normalized as UTC by the reader; session reporting
  converts entries to WIB (UTC+7).
- Config: `config_finex.example.json`
- Costs: spread 20 broker points, adverse slippage 2 points per fill,
  commission 0 (not calibrated from an actual broker statement)
- XAUUSD contract size: 100 oz per lot; research margin leverage: 1:100
- Every evaluation starts with a 50-bar no-entry warm-up and liquidates open
  positions at the evaluation boundary.

## Walk-forward methodology and result

- 7 non-overlapping folds
- 3,000 training bars followed by 1,000 unseen test bars
- 12 predeclared candidates across EMA, score threshold, and SL/TP choices
- Candidate selection uses training data only with a minimum of 20 closed
  training trades and a return/drawdown/profit-factor score.

Aggregate unseen-test result:

| Metric | Result |
|---|---:|
| OOS net profit | +$80.83 |
| Mean fold return | +0.11547% |
| Completed trades | 491 |
| Win rate | 62.93% |
| Profit factor | 1.007 |
| Worst fold drawdown | 11.4905% |
| Modeled transaction cost | $1,412.16 |

The most selected candidate was
`EMA 5/13, scores 0.05/-0.05, SL 20, TP 12` in 4 of 7 folds. Other selected
candidates were `EMA 3/7, SL 30, TP 20` (2 folds) and `EMA 5/13, SL 30,
TP 20` (1 fold). The parameter instability and near-one OOS profit factor do
not justify changing the live configuration.

New York was positive in aggregate but was not positive in every fold. Session

independent dataset.

## Decision

No optimized parameter is promoted to live configuration. The measured OOS
edge is too small relative to cost-model uncertainty, regime coverage, and the
unrecovered final-fold drawdown. Next evidence should come from new data and
calibrated broker fills, not a larger parameter search on this same sample.

## Reproduction

```powershell
python -m aitrader_bot.cli research `
  --config config_finex.example.json `
  --data data/xauusd_5m_merged.csv `
  --train-bars 3000 --test-bars 1000 --step-bars 1000 `
  --warmup-bars 50 --max-candidates 12 --minimum-trades 20 `
  --spread-points 20 --slippage-points 2 `
  --output research_report.json
```
